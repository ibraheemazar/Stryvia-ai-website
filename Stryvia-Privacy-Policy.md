## Who we are

This policy explains how Stryvia ("we", "us", "our") collects and handles personal data when you use stryvia.ai (the "site"). For any question about this policy or your data, contact us at [privacy@stryvia.ai].

## What we collect

We collect only what we need to understand your problem and follow up with you.

Information you give us. When you use the chat on the site, we collect the messages you send and the conversation that results. If you choose to take the next step or request access, we collect the contact details you provide, your name, your email, and optionally your company.

Information collected automatically. When you visit, we collect standard technical and usage data through our analytics: pages viewed, actions taken on the site, approximate location derived from your IP address, device and browser type, the language you use, and similar information that helps us understand how the site is used.

We do not ask for sensitive personal data, and we ask that you do not share it in the chat.

## How we use your data

We use your data to understand the problem you bring, to respond to you, to follow up if you ask us to build something with you, to operate and improve the site, and to understand what visitors need so we can serve them better. We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use it for advertising.

The chat is powered by a third-party AI model provider that processes the content of your conversation in order to respond. We share with that provider only what is needed to generate the response.

## How the chat conversations are handled

Conversations on the site, including those that do not lead to contact, are stored so that we can understand what visitors are looking for and improve how we help. Where a conversation includes contact details, it is linked to your record so that the person who follows up has the context.

## Analytics and cookies

We use analytics to understand how the site is used, including which pages and features draw attention and where visitors encounter friction. This may include session recordings with personal form fields masked so that we never capture the details you type into a contact form. We use cookies and similar technologies for this. On your first visit you will be asked for your consent to non-essential analytics, and you can withdraw it at any time. Essential cookies needed to make the site work do not require consent.

We use a small set of trusted providers to run the site: a hosting provider, a database provider, an AI model provider that powers the chat, and an analytics provider. ## How long we keep your data

## Your rights

Under the Kingdom's Personal Data Protection Law you have the right to know what personal data we hold about you, to access it, to ask us to correct it, to ask us to delete it, and to withdraw consent you have given. To exercise any of these, contact us at [privacy@stryvia.ai] and we will respond within the period the law requires.

## Children

The site is intended for businesses and adults. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
